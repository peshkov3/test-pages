It was reported on Saturday morning that 6 very dangerous prisoners escaped from the Barbeton Maximum prison on Friday night.

According to Barbeton Times, a local newspaper, the prisoners escaped from the prison just before midnight and the police are still searching for them.

Loading...

This according to reports, happened while two male prison guards who were working night shift, gave each other blows jobs. According to Barbeton Times, a cctv footage which is still held by the police, shows the two going into a bathroom while holding hands, and also coming out wiping theirs mouths.

“A disciplinary probe which will involve the suspension of both officers has been initiated. We condemn this extremely offensive incident for the people of this province, given that were dealing with public servants on duty who should be protecting the lives and security of our people. comment Barbeton Prison Manager, Mr. Elias Mboweni.

These prisoners may be armed and very dangerous, so the community is advised to inform the SAPS if they spot them, and not take matters into their own hands.
