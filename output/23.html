Did you manage to watch the latest Game of Thrones premiere yet? If not, I honestly don’t know what you’re doing here, but hey, it’s your life, and you can have as many spoilers as you want. Speaking of spoilers, some time ago there was a leak that Ed Sheeran would be making a guest appearance in the new season of Game of Thrones. Apparently, not everyone got the memo, because when he did appear, people went NUTS! I’m not a big fan of his, because I can’t stand that whiny little voice. To each their own, I guess, but I had no idea the interned hated this ginger dude with a passion! What is up with that? Anyway, as soon as he was introduced in the episode, tweets started flying, memes started brewing, and somehow the redhead became relevant again, but not thanks to his campfire song.

 

Again, I have no idea what he did to all these people, but the hilarity ensued. So much, in fact, that he’s gone off Twitter… AGAIN! Man, this is just so cruel, and I freaking love it!
